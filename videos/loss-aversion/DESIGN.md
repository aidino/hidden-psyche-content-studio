# Design System: Loss Aversion (TikTok Hook)

## Style Prompt
A bold, high-energy "Social/Playful" style tailored for a 9:16 vertical hook video. Visuals are minimal but punchy, focusing entirely on typography. The vibe is modern, energetic, and slightly alarming (reflecting the "loss/threat" concept), but optimistic at the end.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#111111` (Deep dark canvas)
- **Primary Text**: `#FFFFFF` (Clean white for readability)
- **Accent 1 (Loss/Threat)**: `#FF3366` (Hot pink/red for emphasis on "loss", "worse", "threats", "defeat")
- **Accent 2 (Gain/Solution)**: `#00E5FF` (Cyan/teal for "finding", "rewards", "smarter")

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Outfit`, `sans-serif` (Bold, clean, geometric—great for social). Weights: 700 to 900.

## What NOT to Do
- **Do NOT** use small text (minimum 64px for mobile readability).
- **Do NOT** show more than one caption group at a time.
- **Do NOT** use static text; every word should have at least a subtle karaoke animation, with key words bouncing/popping.
- **Do NOT** use slow, lingering fades. Entrances and exits should be snappy (under 0.3s).

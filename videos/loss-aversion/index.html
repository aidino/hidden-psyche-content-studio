Why does losing
10 dollars feel
so much worse
than finding 10
dollars? That's loss
aversion. Our brains
are hardwired to
prioritize threats over
rewardsa survival mechanism
from our ancestors.
This invisible bias
traps us in
failing situations, like
the sunk cost
fallacy, just to
avoid admitting defeat.
It even makes
us overvalue things
simply because we
own them! By
understanding these emotional
patterns, you can
break free and
make smarter life
decisions.

# Tone Voice — Wealth Is What You Don't See
# Date: 2026-04-12

```
TONE: intellectual
VOICE DIRECTION: This script speaks to the viewer who suspects that the people with the loudest lifestyles aren't actually the ones with the most. The tone is calm and revealing — not shaming people who spend, but exposing the invisible psychology behind why we confuse display with stability. It should feel like having the curtain pulled back on something everyone sees but nobody questions.
KEY PHRASES TO USE: "Wealth is what you don't see", "The person with the $100,000 car is not the rich one — the person who could buy it but didn't is", "Psychology calls this...", "Your mind already understands something most people don't"
AVOID: Financial advice or specific investment recommendations, shaming people for spending, glorifying extreme frugality, "money is evil" framing
```
